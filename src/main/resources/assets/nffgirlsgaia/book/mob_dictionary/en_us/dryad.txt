The dryad is the spirit of the trees, found frequently in the Forest biomes. They are neutral mobs.
<NEWLINE>
To get friendly with a Dryad, you must give them Regenerate III effect of over 10 seconds with the splash potion, and
then give them items. They love Cureberries, Randomberries, XP Berries and Mysterious Petals, and also receives
Spore Blossoms and other flowers.
