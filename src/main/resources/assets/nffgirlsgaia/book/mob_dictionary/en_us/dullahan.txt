The Dullahan is a scary creature which hold their own head, found in the Plateau biomes. They hold a Cursed Metal Sword
as weapon, but the sword don't have any special effects.
<NEWLINE>
The method of getting friendly with the Dullahan is the same as their HMaG variants. You can give them items while carrying
an Undead Affinity effect. They love Soul Cake Slice, and also receive Soul Apple and Soul Powder.
